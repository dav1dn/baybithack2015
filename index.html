what's a bay bit hack?

We're the first collegiate hackathon dedicated to blockchain and cryptocurrencies. Spend a weekend with us and join the ranks of a rapidly growing number of outstanding developers and entrepeneurs building on the future of the way we look at money. No experience with bitcoin or cryptocurrency technology is needed.

Bay Bit Hack is a collaborate effort between students at UC Berkeley, Stanford University, and the college cryptocurrency network. All students are welcome!

but wait! what's a bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a currency. It's a technology. It's a protocol of the future.

Bitcoin is a decentralized peer to peer online currency that can be sent and received by anyone in the world, relying on computers to control the transfer and creation of money without human intervention. This results in negligible transaction fees, no government corruption, and lightning-speed transfer of your digital property.
